Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

1. What is IWINIT?

IWINIT is a platform that brings together opportunities from trusted organisations in one place. We feature jobs, bursaries, discounts, referral programmes, competitions and business opportunities to make them easier to discover.

2. Do I apply through IWINIT?

No. IWINIT does not process applications or registrations. Each opportunity includes a Read More or Apply Now button that takes you directly to the official website.

3. Are the opportunities free to view?

Yes. Browsing opportunities on IWINIT is completely free. We simply help you find opportunities from different organisations in one convenient place.

4. How often are new opportunities added?

We regularly update the website with new jobs, bursaries, competitions, discounts, referral programmes and business opportunities as they become available.

5. How do I know if an opportunity is still available?

We do our best to keep listings up to date. Before applying, always visit the official website using the provided link to confirm the latest information, requirements and closing dates.

6. Can I share opportunities with friends and family?

Absolutely. We encourage you to share opportunities that may benefit others. Helping someone discover a new opportunity can make a real difference.

7. Does IWINIT guarantee that I will be selected?

No. Selection decisions are made entirely by the organisation offering the opportunity. IWINIT only provides information and links to official sources.

8. How can I stay updated?

Follow our social media pages and visit the website regularly to discover the latest opportunities across all categories.

9. How can I contact IWINIT?

If you have questions, suggestions or would like to report an issue with a listing, please use our Contact Us page or connect with us through our social media channels.
