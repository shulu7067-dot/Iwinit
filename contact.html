Frequently Asked Questions

How It Works

1. What happens after I submit a referral?

Once you submit a referral, our team reviews the information to ensure it is complete. If everything is correct, the referral is sent to the relevant partner company for processing.

2. How can I track my referral?

You will receive a unique reference code after submitting your referral. Visit the Track Lead page and enter your reference code to check its latest status.

3. What statuses can my referral have?

Received – We have received your referral.
Under Review – Information is being checked.
Sent to Partner – The referral has been forwarded.
Approved – referral has qualified for commission.
Declined – The referral did not go through and does not qualify for a commission.

4. When do I get paid?

Payment is made only after the partner company confirms that your referral has been successfully approved and all their requirements have been met.

5. How will I receive my commission?

Approved commissions are paid via Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) directly into your nominated bank account. Once your lead is approved our team will contact you through the contact details you submitted when you submitted the lead requesting tour banking details

6. How long do payments take?

Payment times vary depending on the partner company. Once we receive payment from the partner, your commission will be processed according to our payment schedule.

7. What if my referral is declined?

If the referral does not signup for the partner's services the referral does not qualify, unfortunately no commission will be payable.

8. Can I submit more than one referral?

Yes. There is no limit to the number of referrals you can submit, provided each referral is genuine and meets the offer requirements.

9. Who can I contact if I need help?

Contact us using the form above, WhatsApp us, or send us an email. Our support team will gladly assist you.
